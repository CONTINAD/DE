Questions, answered
The machine, in plain English
How do I get the DELL?
Hold at least 500,000 $DELL in your wallet. Every cycle the machine airdrops tokenized DELL straight to you, sized to your share of the pot. Nothing to claim — it just shows up.
What funds the airdrops?
pump.fun creator fees. Each cycle the bot claims the fees, buys DELL on Jupiter, and ships it out. It can only ever spend what it has provably claimed — the dev's SOL is never touched.
Is this real Dell stock?
The concept buys tokenized DELL equity (a Token-2022 asset on Solana, 1 token ≈ 1 share) on the open market. This page is a demo with simulated numbers — wire the live bot + feed to make it real.
Do I have to claim or stake?
No. No claiming, no staking, no lockups, no wallet connection required to receive. Hold the coin in any Solana wallet and the DELL is airdropped to you automatically.
How big is my cut?
It's proportional — your bag ÷ all qualified holders' bags, applied to every drop. Bigger bag, bigger slice. Use the calculator above to estimate yours.
Is this affiliated with Dell?
No. This is an independent, memecoin-style concept for entertainment — not affiliated with, endorsed by, or representing Dell Technologies, and not financial advice.
